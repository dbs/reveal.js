Three main tools at Laurentian

  • Canadian Company Capabilities
  • Business Source Complete
  • Lexis-Nexis company profiles
ABI/Inform Trade & Industry mostly indexes news about companies; not company profiles.

Available via

  • laurentian.ca -> Library -> Research databases
  • laurentian.ca -> Library -> Research guides -> Sports Administration

Canadian Company Capabilities

  • canada.ca -> Business -> Research and business intelligence -> Directories of Canadian companies -> Search the Canadian companies database
  • Maintained by Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada
  • > 60,000 companies (self-registered)
  • Detailed search offers location / size / sales / NAICS code, etc
  • Results may include contact info, revenue, executives & employee count, subsidiary info
"Self-registered" does mean the data may be stale, inaccurate, and incomplete. Example: NAICS code 722410 - "Drinking Places (Alcoholic Beverages)" turns up 6 results across Canada(!) Example: McGill University

Business Source Complete

  • Includes profiles on ~35,000 Canadian companies
  • Search and filter by location, revenue, employees, NAICS code
  • Results include contact info, revenue, executives & employee count, subsidiary info

LexisNexis Academic

  • Includes profiles on >100,000 Canadian companies
  • Search and filter by location, ticker symbol, DUNS number
  • Some detailed company profiles, aggregated from multiple sources (LN Corporate Affliations, Morningstar, Dun & Bradstreet, S&P, Worldscope)
  • Best used to look up a specific company
